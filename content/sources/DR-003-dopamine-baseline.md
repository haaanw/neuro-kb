## Verdict (one line)

The "dopamine peaks deplete your baseline / comedown" narrative is **not supported** for
healthy people doing ordinary high-stimulation activities. It is real only in the narrower,
clinical sense of dopaminergic hypofunction in chronic addiction — plus anti-reward/stress
adaptations. Reduced motivation is better explained as altered **effort-cost** (Salamone)
than a drained pleasure tank; dopamine drives **wanting**, not **liking** (Berridge).
